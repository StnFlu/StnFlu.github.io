Featured Projects

Inside Us

Inside Us is an uncomprimising dungeon crawler. Play as the lone white blood cell and explore the body making sure to defeat the infection before the you and the body dies.

This game was created as part of the Univerty of Lincoln's 2021 Summer Game Jam. Recieving 1st place from community votes. Inside Us was created over a 48 hour time span, in the Unity game engine using fully original assets. The game can be found here .
